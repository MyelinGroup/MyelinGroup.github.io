Our misson

Myelin is a place that should have always existed but never has. Thatâ€™s why we are building it ground up, starting from a hacker house.

We want to conduct NeuroTech research that breaks the mold of traditional innovation and academic publishing.

We want to build sci-fi dreams that fall outside the start-up model. We believe that ordinary citizens should have the resources to realize their great ideas.

We are a collective of highly talented, multi-disciplinary, and zealous individuals who want to build the next generation of NeuroTech: from non-invasive EEG based Ready-Player-1 prototypes and petri-dish neurons that can play Atari video games, to psychedelic qualia computing and next gen brain stimulation technology. We want to bring together the great young minds to achieve what nobody can achieve alone.

If you believe in our mission and want to be part of this future, join our cause. The Myelin Team
